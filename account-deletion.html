Account Deletion Request

This page explains how users of Log Pose: OP Card Tracker can request deletion of their account and associated data.

How to Request Account Deletion

You can request account deletion in one of the following ways:

  • Directly inside the app (if the feature is available)
  • By contacting us via email

Email us at: donut.dev.inc@gmail.com
Subject: Account Deletion Request

What Data Will Be Deleted

  • Your account information (email, authentication data)
  • Your collection data
  • Any stored user-related data

Data Retention

Some data may be retained for a limited time for legal, security, or fraud prevention purposes. After that, it will be permanently deleted.

Processing Time

Account deletion requests are processed within 3–7 business days.

Contact

If you have any questions, contact us at:
donut.dev.inc@gmail.com
